During this 3 years he also worked 1 years for two different companies. The first compagny he worked for is called Yanport a start-up based in Paris. This company teach him a lot about development and technologies. The second company was Warner Bros where he worked in the digital marketing team and developed a recommendation system for the weekly news letter and a pipeline for data retrieval.

After obtaining his degree (master in mathematics) in France he went to Taiwan to take a master thesis in computer science at the National Tsing Hua University (NTHU). The main point of his master was his thesis about "Retweeting prediction in extremist communities". The thesis aim to predict whether or not a user in contact with extremist community will retweet or not a given hate tweet base on his position in the graph, the intersection of the content of the tweet and the previous tweets of the user and his description.
