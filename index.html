Melvyn grew up in France a kingdom not that far from Piltover, where the Cheese King and the Queen Baguette are ruling the country in tyranny. One day, during his high school after refusing to eat some cheese for breakfast, the imperial guards came to arrest him. Not eating cheese at every meal is a capital crime in France and the sentence was irrevocable, Melvyn had to be drown in wine without trial. The day before the drowning, while Melvyn was in his cell, he heard some fights coming from the corridor, and then few seconds after a long silence, a rescue team composed of Vi, Hecarim, and led by Nami, appeared in front of the cell. Nami use the key to open the gate, looked at Melvyn right in the eyes and said: "Can you stop feeding? I'm sick of carrying you bronze 5". They then escaped the jail insulting each other.

After this experience Melvyn changed his identity to Melvynator, finished his high school, and promised to himself that he will free his country of the Baguette Queen and the Cheese King.

Melvyn has a huge appetite for learning. So he went on to study for two years in a "classe préparatoire" where he learned some white magic. After this he went to study for three years at a white magic school (an engineering school "EISTI") near Paris to improve his skills in applied white magic. During this three years his main focus was runic magic (Mathematics) with a special focus on arcane (graph theory)

While studying, Melvyn realized that he will never be able to beat the King Cheese and the Queen Baguette only with white magic, so he took an irreversible path: he went to work in companies to learn some black magic. The first company he worked for was Yanport, a start-up based in Paris. This company taught him a lot about d(a)emon summoning (development and technologies). The second company was Warner Bros where he worked in the digital marketing team and developed a spell to harass his opponents every time (recommendation system for the weekly news letter) and witchcraft (pipeline for data retrieval).

Melvyn was already corrupted by all the black magic learned but he still received his degree in white magic (master in Mathematics). During the ceremony Melvyn was so torn apart because of the fight between white and black magic inside him (black magic ∩ white magic = ∅), that he was thrown into the void (Taiwan). At first Melvyn was afraid, but he had no choice, so he chosed to take a master thesis in void magic (computer science) at the void school (National Tsing Hua University "NTHU"). The main point of his master was his thesis about "Minions of the Queen Baguette detection". ("Retweeting prediction in extremist communities" - The aim of the thesis is to predict whether or not a user in contact with an extremist community will retweet or not retweet a given tweet that contains hate speech related content based on their position in the graph, the intersection of the content of the tweet, and the previous tweets of the user, and their profile description).

In his spare time Melvyn is part of the swimming team in his university, he also likes to travel and discover new cultures; he is a veteran player of League of Legends, Age of Empires 2, Heroes of Might and Magic.

His dreams are simple:
  • Having his revenge on the Cheese King and Baguette Queen
  • Reach his old platinum ranking
  • Build useful and meaningful tools for people that bring joy and happiness.
And as Melvyn usually says:
